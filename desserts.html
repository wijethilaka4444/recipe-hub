🎯 Baking Tips & Tricks

🌡️ Temperature Matters

Always bring eggs and butter to room temperature for better mixing and texture in your baked goods.

⚖️ Measure Accurately

Use a kitchen scale for precise measurements, especially for flour and sugar, to ensure consistent results.

🥄 Don't Overmix

Mix just until ingredients are combined to avoid tough, dense cakes and cookies.

🔥 Oven Temperature

Use an oven thermometer to ensure accurate temperature. Most ovens run hot or cold.
